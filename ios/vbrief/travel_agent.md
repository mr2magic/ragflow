```
* Phone Number
* Email

* Travel Dates available (from)

---
1) What kind of trip are you thinking of?
* Where would you like to explore?
* Want to elaborate on where your imagination is taking you?
* What kind of vibe are you looking for?
* What activities are you interested in?
* Are you interested in eco-friendly or sustainable travel options?
---
2) Booking Details
* What would you like included in your booking?
* What is your trip budget?
* What resort/hotel star rating do you prefer?
* If applicable, what size car rental will you need?
* Anything else you'd like to share on your budget?
---
Party Details
* Size of Travel Party
* Our party includes:
* Traveling for a special occasion?
* Do you need a crib in your room or any other special accommodations?
* How many children and what ages (at time of travel)?
* How many teens & what ages (at time of travel)?
* How many Seniors?
* Please elaborate on the kind of accommodation your traveler(s) require?
---
3) Final Thoughts?
* What else would you like us to know about your party, budget and/or travel preferences?
* Are we missing anything? What else would you like us to know?
```
